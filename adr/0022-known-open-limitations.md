# ADR-0022: Known open limitations

## Status

Accepted (pointer record)

## Context

The design record throughout names real, current gaps rather than leaving them as implicit TODOs.

## Decision

Keep the known open limitations named explicitly and maintained in their own document rather than scattered as implicit TODOs.

## Consequences

- These are real, current gaps — named rather than left as implicit TODOs. They live in their own document, [KNOWN_LIMITATIONS.md](../KNOWN_LIMITATIONS.md), alongside the scope limits stated at the module level throughout this record.
